We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments. Online payments can be made securely through our payment gateway.
Is there a minimum stay requirement? ‚ñº
Yes, we have a minimum stay requirement of one month. This ensures stability for all our residents and helps maintain our community atmosphere.

Yes, most of our room packages include three meals a day. We offer both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Specific meal plans can be discussed during booking.

Absolutely! We encourage prospective residents to visit us and take a tour of our facilities. Please contact us to schedule a visit during our working hours.
